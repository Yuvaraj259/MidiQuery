About MediQuery

MediQuery helps patients and caregivers quickly understand their medicines. No jargon. No confusion.

🔍

Three ways to identify

Type the name, upload a photo of the pill or packaging, or enter the barcode from the label.

⏰

Timing guidance

Clearly shows whether to take before or after food, morning or night, and how often.

⚠️

Safety first

Highlights critical side effects, drug interactions, and items to avoid while taking the medicine.

💊

Alternatives

Shows generic versions and alternative medicines so you can have an informed conversation with your doctor.

🌐

Multilanguage

Available in English, Hindi, and Kannada — switch languages anytime from the top navigation.

🔊

Voice Assistant

Tap the speaker button after identifying a medicine to hear the full summary read aloud in your chosen language.
